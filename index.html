Canon Specials

Monsters: 103 Mercies Dragon Damnation (2024)

This is an adaptation of a one-shot, that was written by Oda in 1994, before he started working on One Piece.

Despite being a standalone, its main lead is part of the One Piece canon.

Viewable on Netflix(25min)

One Piece Film Strong World: Episode 0 (2010)

This is an adaptation of the one-shot "Chapter 0", which was written by Oda and released alongside one of the Marineford chapters.

The events of this episode are canon & also good to have as context going into Movie #10 (One Piece Film: Strong World).

⚠️ For some reason they thought it would be a good idea to basically spoil the majority of the movie in the preview for this episode... So be careful & skip about a quarter of the way into the video, and then backtrack to 1:45
🎵
Important: For better audio quality, go to Audio → Audio Track → and change it to "Japanese 2.0 DVD - [Japanese]"

Warship Island Arc

Eps. 54-61

This is the first filler arc in One Piece and follows the Straw Hats rescuing a girl and a dragon. The person they throw overboard, on the way up Reverse Mountain, is one of the antagonists of the arc.

Episode 54 (until 7:45) and Episode 61 (from 13:20 onward) are canon material!
